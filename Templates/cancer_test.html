Skin Cancer Test

Skin cancer is the abnormal growth of skin cells, often caused by prolonged exposure to ultraviolet radiation from the sun or tanning beds. Early detection through regular skin checks and prompt treatment is crucial for successful management. Common types include basal cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, and melanoma, with melanoma being the most dangerous form.
